  • Class: Sales Promotion

Role: Project Leader


Project: For the final project in my sales promotion class at the Fashion Institute of Technology, we were asked to develop an integrated marketing campaign for an upcoming product release. My group that I was chosen to lead was given video game Evolve.


We started our project by conducting research about the video game market and its consumers. Next we turned to developing our goals that would help launch the new product. We used our research to come up with a strategy to reach our target consumer. The project allowed us to use any type of media and promotion, so we had lots of creative freedom. I lead our team by delegating different parts of the plan to each team member, and asked them to develop the creative concepts based on our strategy. Take a look at the presentation below.
