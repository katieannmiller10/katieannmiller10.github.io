Role: Project Leader


Project: In my media planning class at the Fashion Institute of Technology, I was asked to lead a team in analyzing the media of a fashion brand. We were given Michael Kors and told to research the brand, its competition, and media. From our research, we had to decide how effective Michael Kors' media tactics were, and give a few ideas for future growth. Michael Kors spends over half of its media budget on advertising in magazines, so our main suggestion was to reallocate the budget and increase the brand's digital media. Check out the deck below.
